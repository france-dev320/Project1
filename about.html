About

Kuya Korea aims to be the go-to for every Filipino when they crave Korean food. It values quality, affordability, and convenience and makes sure to incorporate all these into its dishes.

With much exposure to Hallyu, Korean food has now become a prominent part of Filipino cravings. With this in mind, Kuya Korea aims not just to become another option, but to become a benchmark that exceeds its customers expectations of a quick Korean fix every time.
